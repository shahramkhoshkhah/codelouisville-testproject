About Black Rose Garden

At Black Rose Garden, we are a passionate and award-winning group of landscape designers, landscape and construction professionals enhancing nature's beauty for over 15 years. Our team of experts love what they do and it shows. Installations are well organized and completed in a timely manner. An appreciation for a variety of plant life, from traditional to exotic and nativescapes creates a unique and diverse portfolio and speaks to our philosophy that no two jobs are the same. Whether you have a small garden or large formal estate, a small commercial office space or a large corporate campus, let us transform your outdoor environment. Not only will it enhance the beauty around you, but it will increase the value of your property. At Black Rose Garden, we believe in pursuing beauty one client at a time. If you are interested in having Black Rose Garden create the landscape and outdoor living area of your dreams, contact us to schedule a complimentary consultation.
